# InScape Cookies Policy

**Effective Date: May 19, 2024**

InScape (“InScape,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our websites, mobile applications, and browser extensions (collectively, the “Services”). This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use. It complements our Privacy Policy, which details broader data handling practices.

## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our Services. They help us recognize your device, remember preferences, and improve functionality. We also use similar technologies, such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage, for comparable purposes.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use
We use the following categories of cookies:

- **Essential Cookies**:
  - **Purpose**: Enable core functionality, such as account login, navigation, and reward tracking.
  - **Examples**: Session cookies for authentication, security cookies for fraud prevention.
  - **Duration**: Typically session-based (deleted after your visit) or short-term (up to 30 days).
  - **Consent**: Not required, as they are necessary for Service operation.

- **Analytics Cookies**:
  - **Purpose**: Collect data on how users interact with our Services (e.g., pages visited, click patterns) to improve performance and user experience.
  - **Examples**: Google Analytics cookies for usage trends, custom analytics for offer relevance.
  - **Duration**: Up to 2 years.
  - **Consent**: Requires your consent, except where data is anonymized.

- **Advertising Cookies**:
  - **Purpose**: Deliver personalized ads and track ad performance, including on third-party sites, based on your browsing history and preferences.
  - **Examples**: Ad network cookies (e.g., Google Ads, affiliate partner pixels) for targeted offers.
  - **Duration**: Up to 2 years.
  - **Consent**: Requires your explicit consent.

- **Functional Cookies**:
  - **Purpose**: Remember your preferences (e.g., favorite stores, language settings) to enhance usability.
  - **Examples**: Preference cookies for customized dashboards.
  - **Duration**: Up to 1 year.
  - **Consent**: Requires your consent.

## 3. Third-Party Cookies
Some cookies are set by third parties, such as:
- **Analytics Providers**: Google Analytics to measure site performance.
- **Advertising Partners**: Affiliate networks and ad platforms to deliver targeted ads.
- **Social Media Platforms**: For sign-in or sharing features (e.g., via Facebook, Google).

These third parties may collect data under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review. We ensure third parties comply with applicable laws (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) through data protection agreements.

## 4. How We Use Cookies
We use cookies to:
- Provide and secure the Services (e.g., authenticate logins, prevent fraud).
- Personalize your experience (e.g., display relevant offers).
- Analyze usage to improve functionality and performance.
- Deliver targeted advertising, with your consent.
- Facilitate Partner tracking for rewards (e.g., affiliate link attribution).

## 5. Your Choices
You can manage cookies through:
- **Cookie Banner**: On your first visit, consent to non-essential cookies or customize preferences. Update choices anytime via the cookie settings link on our website.
- **Browser Settings**: Block or delete cookies in your browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox). Note: Blocking essential cookies may impair Service functionality.
- **Device Settings**: Adjust tracking permissions for our mobile app in your device’s privacy settings.
- **Opt-Out Requests**: Email inquiries@inscapedls.com to opt out of non-essential cookies or tracking.
- **Global Privacy Control (GPC)**: We honor GPC signals to disable advertising cookies automatically.

Disabling cookies may limit personalized features, such as tailored offers or reward tracking.

## 6. International Compliance
We comply with cookie-related laws, including:
- **GDPR/ePrivacy Directive (EU)**: Requiring explicit consent for non-essential cookies.
- **CCPA (USA)**: Allowing opt-out of data sharing via advertising cookies.
- **Australia’s Privacy Act/Spam Act**: Ensuring transparency and consent.
- **New Zealand’s Privacy Act**: Supporting user control over tracking.
- **Canada’s PIPEDA**: Requiring clear consent for data collection.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Cookies Policy to reflect changes in technology or law. We will notify you by revising the effective date and, for material changes, via email or in-Service notices.
